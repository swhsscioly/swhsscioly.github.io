We are the Bobcat Science Olympiad Booster Club. As a 501(c)3 nonprofit organization in the State of Connecticut, we raise funds for the Bobcat Science Olympiad. Our purpose is to enrich the Science Olympiad experience at South Windsor High School. We take care of fundraising so that students can focus on their events and have a fulfililng, memorable, and educational experience in Science Olympiad. Our leadership structure and club bylaws are given below. We meet at South Windsor High School on a quarterly basis. We are always welcome to new members. Prospective members should contact one of the club's officer(s) regarding membership.
