Sponsors

Our sponsors play an integral role in our success. We are always open to sponsorship requests. Sponsoring a meritorious Science Olympiad team entails numerous benefits. Please view our sponsorship levels here:


  • Your company’s logo on side panel of our air trajectory, robot, and electric vehicle (shown in invitational, state, and national tournaments)
  • Your company’s name and logo on our team boxes and equipment (shown in invitational, state, and national tournaments)
  • Your company’s logo on our national’s T-Shirt (gives your company exposure at nationals)
  • All of the benefits of Bobcat Engineer, Bobcat Scientist, and Bobcat Explorer

  • A 100 word caption of your choice provided with your company’s logo on our website and an Instagram post about your company
  • Your company’s name and logo on our poster board - displayed at team expositions and outreach events
  • Your company featured in our annual presentation shown to the South Windsor Board of Education
  • All of the benefits of Bobcat Scientist and Bobcat Explorer

  • Your company’s logo on our website
  • Post on our Instagram about your donation
  • Your company’s logo on 2024-25 team T-Shirt
    • (worn at team events and all tournaments)
  • Your company’s logo on our team banner
  • All of the benefits of Bobcat Explorer

  • Team photos mailed to you at end of season
  • Letter of Appreciation mailed to you with team photos

Checks should be made out to South Windsor High School Science Olympiad and mailed to our school (161 Nevers Road, South Windsor, CT 06074) Prospective sponsors should use the information on the leadership page to get in touch with us. You can also reach out to us on Instagram.
