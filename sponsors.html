Sponsors

Our sponsors play an integral role in our success. We are always open to sponsorship requests. Sponsoring a meritorious Science Olympiad team entails numerous benefits. Our sponsors are:


  1. Recognized on our website's homepage and sponsors page
  2. Displayed on team apparel (worn at all invitational, state, and national tournaments)
  3. Advertised on our team's social media pages
  4. Included in our communications with parents and families

    5. Checks should be made out to South Windsor High School Science Olympiad and mailed to our school (161 Nevers Road, South Windsor, CT 06074) Prospective sponsors should use the information on the contact page to get in touch with us. You can also reach out to us on Instagram.

  5. We look forward to working with you!
