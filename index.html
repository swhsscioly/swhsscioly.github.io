Who Are We

Founded in 2003, the South Windsor High School Science Olympiad is a group of motivated and bright students at South Windsor High School who compete in the nation's most prestigious science tournament. Every year, we assemble two teams and compete at invitational and state tournaments. Science Olympiad is challenging and requires great amounts of dedication and effort from every student. We give up our free time to study science because science inspires us and motivates us.


Our Mission

We push our intellectual limits in the pursuit of knowledge and joy. Throughout the years, we have won numerous tournaments due to the hard work of our team. Recently, our team has won the Connecticut State Science Olympiad tournament two years in a row and has appeared at the national tournament. Our success is a direct result of the work every team member puts in everyday after school. We have also crafted a nationwide alumni network of scientists and engineers who advance the frontiers of science and technology everyday.


Achievements

Over the years, we have won 1st place the CT State Science Olympiad tournament three times and have finished 2nd 14 times. Last season, we won 12 medals. This year, we hope to beat that number as we are always improving. Our team has done very well at invitational tourmaments at prestigious universities, including Cornell, MIT, and Yale. We have also competed in the National Science Olympiad tournament every time we have won the state tournament (3 times).


States 2022

Our team has won the CT State Science Olympiad tournament for the past two years in a row. We are working to win states again this year and in consecutive years. In order to improve, our team competed in and won a medal at the Cornell University Science Olympiad tournament as early as in November of 2023.
